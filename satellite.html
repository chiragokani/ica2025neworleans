Satellite Symposia

International Symposium on Musical Acoustics (ISMA)

International Symposium on Room Acoustics (ISRA)

Two satellite symposia will be held in conjunction with the congress, the International Symposium on Musical Acoustics (ISMA), chaired by Andrew Piacsek, and the International Symposium on Room Acoustics (ISRA), chaired by David Woolworth, which will be combined into a single joint symposium referred to as ISMRA. The joint ISMRA symposium will be held immediately following the close of the ICA, during the week of May 25 at Loyola University. The New Orleans Marriott and Loyola University are connected by the historic St. Charles Avenue Streetcar, which is said to be the oldest continuously operated streetcar line in the world.

Joint ISMA/ISRA sessions will be held on the first day, and in subsequent days ISMA and ISRA will hold parallel sessions but with combined social events. Inexpensive dorm accommodations are being developed at Loyola University for those who prefer to be onsite, and other nearby hotel accommodations are available in the Milan and Touro districts along the streetcar route. Additional information will be made available soon at the website ismra2025.org. Technical program and other details will be available at the end of the summer.
